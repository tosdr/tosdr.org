Thank you!

We would like to thank the individuals and organisations that support the project. Without them, this effort would not be sustainable! Also, special credits to those who helped the project get started:

  • the Unhosted movement
  • The participants of Berlin Hack & Tell at C-Base

In September 2012, we launched a crowdfunding campaign on Indiegogo. Thanks to the efforts of hundreds of people, we managed to reach ten thousand euros and get a grant from Gründer Garage, Google for Entrepreneurs and Siftung Entrepreneurship.

We are also flattered by the regular support we get from small donations! Thanks to the users of flattr!
