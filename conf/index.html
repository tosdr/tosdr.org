Hackathon September 26-27, Berlin

For two days, we are back at fixing the biggest lie on the web together. Our tools need major updates to allow our community to continue submitting reviews and discuss good and bad points on Terms of service and Privacy policies.

If you plan to attend, please give us notice by email to <team at tosdr.org> so we can plan on a bigger venue if need be. If you’re not in Berlin, we have funds available. If you’re in London or Paris and you’re interested, get in touch with us!

Anyone is welcome to join, no matter which programming language you use, there are a lot of things to code and contribute to! Including:

  • A submission system to:
    • fill in a review about a point in ToS&PP
    • fill in a URL to archive to ToS&PP
    • prioritise pending reviews
  • Firefox, Chrome, Safari, Opera and IE extensions
  • Separate build and output Git repositories
  • A localisable output
  • Integrate Justdelete.me and accountkiller.info APIs

Location

Our hackathon is kindly hosted by EndoCode AG, Free Software experts. Their office is at Johannisstrasse 20, 10117 Berlin, Germany.

Practical information

The event is taking place from Friday 16:00 to Saturday 18:00.

Funds are available to cover your travel and accommodation costs to Berlin. Please check with us by email to <buro at tosdr.org>. Food and drinks will be provided by the ToS;DR association.
